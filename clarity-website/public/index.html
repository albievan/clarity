The problem

Budget management
is still broken.

Finance teams spend hundreds of hours each year reconciling spreadsheets, chasing approvals over email, and manually compiling reports that are out of date the moment they're published. There is no single source of truth. Commitments are invisible. Actuals arrive weeks late. Renewal deadlines are missed.

Clarity was built to fix this — not by adding another integration to an ERP system, but by giving every stakeholder in the budget process a role-appropriate, real-time view of where money is going and why.

⊘

No real-time visibility

Budgets live in spreadsheets that are hours or days out of date by the time anyone reads them.

⊘

Approval by email

Approval chains run through inboxes with no audit trail, no escalation, and no accountability.

⊘

Missed renewals

Support and maintenance agreements expire without warning, creating unplanned emergency spend.

⊘

Opaque commitments

Open purchase orders and encumbrances are invisible, making forecasts systematically wrong.

Platform capabilities

Everything a finance team needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

Complete budget visibility

Real-time running balances across every dimension — department, cost centre, project, vendor, and geography. Planned, committed, actual, and remaining — always current.

  • CapEx & OpEx classification
  • Multi-currency with FX snapshots
  • Split cost centre allocation
  • Hierarchical department roll-ups

Configurable approvals

Visual workflow builder with multi-level rules, two-person integrity for high-value items, delegation, and escalation.

Agreement calendar

See every support & maintenance contract on a visual calendar with 90/30/7-day expiry alerts and acknowledgement tracking.

Advanced forecasting

Three projection models — linear run-rate, committed + actuals, manual — with RAG indicators and automatic forecast alerts.

Cross-period trend analysis

Compare budget performance across up to 5 fiscal years simultaneously, normalised for FX, with federated access to archived data.

Bottom-up budget intake

Budget Requestors submit proposals for Department Head review before they reach the formal budget — a structured, auditable intake layer.

Purchase order lifecycle

Full PO management with line items, goods receipt, partial delivery, dispute handling, and automatic encumbrance release on close.

AI justification assistant

Anthropic Claude API or local Ollama — helps users write persuasive, professional budget justifications with a single click.

Immutable audit trail

Every budget change, approval, rejection, and cost amendment is cryptographically hashed and retained for 7 years. Every rejection requires a reason. Every cost change requires a justification. Finance Controllers get a full-featured audit log viewer with before/after diffs.

  • Mandatory rejection reason library
  • Cost change justification modal
  • Searchable audit log viewer UI
  • SOX & GDPR compliant by design

How Clarity works

One platform.
The complete budget lifecycle.

01

Request

Budget Requestors submit proposals to their Department Head — with AI-assisted justifications, supporting documents, and vendor selections from the pre-configured register.

02

Approve

Multi-level approval workflows with configurable rules, two-person integrity for high-value items, delegation, automatic escalation, and structured rejection reasons.

03

Commit

Approved budgets generate purchase orders. Encumbrances are tracked in real time. Goods receipt converts committed spend to confirmed actuals automatically.

04

Track

Real-time dashboards, cross-period trend analysis, commitment ageing reports, and budget utilisation forecasting — all in one place, always live.

05

Close

Formal period close-down with soft lock, hard lock, carry-over rules, and a Period Close Summary Report. Every step dual-approved and permanently audited.
