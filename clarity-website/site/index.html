Platform features

Everything your finance team needs, nothing they don't

Clarity is purpose-built for budget management. No bloat, no consultants required to configure it, no six-month implementation.

Multi-level approval workflows
Configure approval rules by department, budget type, and spend threshold. Two-person integrity for high-value items. Full delegation when approvers are out of office.
Real-time running balances
Planned → Committed → Actuals → Remaining calculated in real time. PO encumbrances move automatically to actuals on goods receipt. Never a stale number.
Agreement & contract tracking
Track every support and maintenance agreement with automated end-date calculation, recurring cost projection with % increases, and an agreement calendar with 90-day expiry alerts.
AI-assisted justifications
Budget Owners write a rough note; Clarity's AI rewrites it into a persuasive, professional justification. Runs on your own Ollama instance or the Anthropic API — you choose.
Budget intake & requestor flow
Individual contributors submit budget item requests. Department Heads approve for inclusion. Budget Owners consolidate into the formal budget. Every step tracked and audited.
Cross-period trend analysis
Compare budget vs. actual performance across up to 5 fiscal years simultaneously. FX normalised to base currency using historical snapshot rates. Federated across archived periods.
How it works

From request to approved budget in minutes

Clarity structures the entire budget lifecycle into a clear, auditable flow that your team will actually follow.

1
Set up your reference data
Import your departments, cost centres, vendors, and cost categories in bulk from Excel. The guided setup wizard walks the Finance Controller through every step before a single budget is created.
2
Request, review, consolidate
Team members submit budget item requests. Department Heads approve for inclusion. Budget Owners consolidate approved items into a formal budget submission — with full traceability from request to approved line.
3
Multi-level approval
The budget moves through your configured approval levels. Each approver sees the full justification, cost history, vendor details, and any AI-improved rationale. Rejection reasons are mandatory and reportable.
4
Track spend in real time
Raise POs, receive goods, enter actuals. The running balance updates instantly. Finance Controllers see every department's position in one dashboard. Period close is a structured 7-step process.
